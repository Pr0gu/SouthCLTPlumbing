### CRITICAL (must fix)
1. **No coupons / active offers** — WyattWorks has 6 active coupons with expiration dates. Full Spectrum has "Same Day Hot Water or $500 credit." We have NOTHING. **Biggest single conversion lever.**
2. **No Google rating displayed** — Full Spectrum shows "4.9 Google Rating" prominently. We don't show any rating.
3. **No guarantee language** — Not ONE competitor has a strong warranty visible, but we should be the first: "Find it or you don't pay" or "100% satisfaction guarantee" or "1-year warranty on all repairs."
4. **No live chat** — Morris-Jenkins has live chat, WyattWorks has live chat. We don't. Fixable with Tawk.to or Crisp (free).
5. **No text message contact option** — Morris-Jenkins explicitly offers text. Nobody in their competitive set offers this prominently.
6. **No zip code finder** — Benjamin Franklin (national) has this. Qualifies leads instantly. Worth building.

8. **No neighborhood landmark copy** — Plumbing Techs mentions Queens Road and Freedom Park. Makes content hyper-local. We should mention Myers Park Country Club, SouthPark Mall area, Waverly, Stonecrest.
9. **No financing / payment plans** — WyattWorks and E.R. Services both offer financing. Big-ticket plumbing (whole-house repipe, water heater replacement) benefits from this.
10. **No founder/owner photo + bio** — E.R. Services has David Parker. Makes brand feel human. Bogdan is a selling point (Ukrainian immigrant story, hands-on owner).
11. **No media/press mentions** — E.R. Services references "In the Press" and "Radio Appearances" for authority.
12. **No longevity claim** — All 5 competitors display their years in business prominently. We should show "Serving Charlotte Since 2020" somewhere visible.
13. **No service-area pages** — Plumbing Techs has 11+ per-neighborhood pages for SEO. We have one big list. Can add dedicated /myers-park, /southpark, /ballantyne pages later.
14. **No emergency response time commitment** — "Typically 30-45 minutes" should be a visible promise, not buried.

### NICE TO HAVE
15. **No membership program** — WyattWorks has Priority Perks+, E.R. Services has memberships. Could offer "ArtFix Care Club" for recurring customers.
16. **No community involvement / CSR** — "Do Good With Dave" for E.R. Services is a unique differentiator.
